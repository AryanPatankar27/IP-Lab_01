About Amazon: Pioneering the Future of Commerce

Amazon is more than just an e-commerce giant; we're innovators, dreamers, and customer-obsessed pioneers. Our journey began in 1994 as an online bookstore, and today, we've transformed into a global powerhouse that's reshaping how the world shops, reads, computes, and entertains.

Our History: From Books to Everything

Started in Jeff Bezos' garage, Amazon has grown from a small online bookstore to the world's largest online retailer. Our timeline is marked by bold moves, technological innovations, and a relentless focus on customer satisfaction.

Our Mission: Customer Obsession

At Amazon, our mission is simple yet profound: to be Earth's most customer-centric company. This drives every decision we make, from developing new technologies to expanding our product offerings. We strive to offer our customers the lowest possible prices, the best available selection, and the utmost convenience.
